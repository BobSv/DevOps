M1RAG3

DJ/Producer. Owner of Dig Out Your Soul Records


Born in 1988 on San Salvador, El Salvador, Guitar player, DJ and Producer. Dig Out Your Soul Records Label owner, M1RAG3 (also known under the moniker "Ricky Mirage") is best known for his eclectic Djing and versatile style moving seamlessly from one genre to another to take everyone on a musical journey while taking control of the dancefloor.

Education

  • UP-Universidad de Palermo: Media & Entertainment Management
  • EMBA-Escuela de Musica de Buenos Aires: Music Production
  • ITMC- Professional Musician (Guitar)
  • TECSON- Professional Live & Recording Sound Technician
  • DRMTM Lessons- Ableton Live Music Production Intensive Courses
